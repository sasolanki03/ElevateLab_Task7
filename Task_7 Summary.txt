----------------------------------Task 7: Identify and Remove Suspicious Browser Extensions-----------------------------------

Objective: Learned to identify and remove potentially harmful browser extensions using Chrome.

Tools Used: Google Chrome

Extensions Reviewed:

-Chrome Remote Desktop – Safe 
-Google Docs Offline – Safe 
-Sound Booster - Increase Volume Up – Suspicious (Removed)
-Volume Master – Suspicious  (Removed)

Actions Taken:
-Opened chrome://extensions/
-Checked permissions and reviews
-Removed Sound Booster and Volume Master due to excessive permissions
-Restarted browser for changes
